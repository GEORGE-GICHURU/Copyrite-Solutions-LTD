Shipping & Returns

Shipping Guidelines

Due to high order volume at this time, shipping may be slightly delayed. We will do our best to process your order as quick as possible.

Copyrite Solutions only ships within Kenya. Please note that delays may occur due to weather or other unforeseen circumstance.

Product And Gift Boxes

Orders placed on in-stock items before 12 p.m. Monday through Friday will ship that day. Orders places after 12 p.m. Monday through Thursday will ship the following day. Orders placesd after 12p.m. on Friday will ship the following Monday.

All shipping charges listed below are based on UPS Ground orders and will arrive with 2-7 business days (Sundays not included).

We guarantee delivery only to the address you provide. Shipments that you re-direct and shipments to incorrect or incomplete addresses cannot be guaranteed to deliver on time. We do not deliver to P.O. boxes.

Shipping Charges

Free shipping is offered on all UPS Ground orders over KES6000.

Standard Group Shipping

Orders with a final subtotal from KES0 - KES9800 ship with a flat-rate of KES900 per address.

Second Day Shipping

Add KES2200 per address.

Next Day Air

Add KES3500 per address.

Return Policies

At Copyrite solutions, we take great care and pride in selecting the highest quality products for our customers. If you are not satisfied with your purchase or an item is damaged, we will find another solution. Please note that returns for items purchased online are not accepted at our stores.

In-Store Purchases

All products are eligible for an exchange within 14days of receipt. Gift cards are non-refundable. If you have any questions, please contact our Guest Relations teams
